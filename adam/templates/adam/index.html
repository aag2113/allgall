Ex Machina

2016.04.24

If you haven't seen "Ex Machina" yet then I highly suggest you give it a watch. The film is a sci-fi thriller that explores the potentially destructive consequences of artificial intelligence. Seriously, go watch it... specifically on DVD.

Why DVD? Well, the film itself is great, but if 90 minutes of entertainment is what you're after then all you are going to need is the DVD menu screen. A true achievement in the medium, the DVD menu screen features a serene animation of particles floating through space, periodically connecting to one another, like synapses in the aether. Hyperbole aside, I watched it for about 20 minutes.

In fact, I was so taken with the effect that I decided to try to recreate it in Python!

Here is the result, as well as the source.

And just like that, my first screen-saver.
